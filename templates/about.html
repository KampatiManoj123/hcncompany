Our Story

Committed to a Cleaner Tomorrow

HCN (Hygiene Care Novelty) was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make high-quality cleaning and hygiene products accessible to every home, workplace, and commercial space in India.

We manufacture all our products in our own state-of-the-art facility, ensuring every bottle that leaves our factory meets the strictest quality standards. From dishwash liquids to bathroom cleaners, every HCN product is formulated with safe, effective, and eco-conscious ingredients.
